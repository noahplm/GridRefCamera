GridRefCamera

Take or choose a photo, capture GPS, convert it to a 10-figure British National Grid reference, and stamp it onto the image.

Waiting for photo and GPS.
Download stamped photo On iPhone, after downloading, use Share then Save Image if needed.
GPS warning thresholds

Good: ±10 m or better. Warning: ±10-25 m. Weak: worse than ±25 m. The warning is stamped on the image if accuracy is weak.

This needs to be hosted over HTTPS, for example GitHub Pages, for GPS to work on iPhone.
